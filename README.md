# 🕵️ Digital Detective

> A digital detective mystery where every clue matters.

## 📖 Overview

**Digital Detective** is a text-based detective game built in Python for **Code in Place 2026**.

A valuable company laptop has mysteriously disappeared, and it's your job to uncover the truth. Search for evidence, interview suspects, analyze security logs, and connect the dots before making your final accusation.

But be careful—one wrong accusation could let the real culprit walk free.

---

## 🎮 Gameplay

As a digital detective, you'll:

* 🔍 Investigate the office
* 👥 Interview suspects
* 📹 Review security logs
* 📝 Collect and review clues
* ⚖️ Make a final accusation

Your detective skills will determine whether justice is served.

### Sample Gameplay

```text
==================================================
DIGITAL DETECTIVE
The Missing Laptop Mystery
==================================================

What would you like to do?

1. Inspect the office
2. Interview Alice
3. Interview Bob
4. Check security logs
5. View collected clues
6. Make accusation
7. Quit
```
